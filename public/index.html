Midcourse Correction
Midcourse correction is the option to order new aligners during treatment, if clinical result have deviated to such an extent that the aligners no longer fit. For Mid-course corrections and/or refinements. we offer free aligners up to 20% of your case total. For example, for 20 aligner case , your are eligible for up to 4 free aligners. Any Midcourse correction or refinements aligners in excess of your free aligner allotment will be $19 per aligner, and no discounts are applicable.
Refinements
Refinements refer to extra trays given to patients who aren’t progressing in accordance with their treatment plan. Refinements will add a couple of extra months to your treatment. For mid-course corrections and/or refinements, we offer free aligners up to 20% of your case total. For example, for a 20 aligner case, you are eligible for up to 4 free aligners. Any mid-course correction or refinement aligners in excess of your free aligner allotment will be $19 per aligner, and no discounts are applicable.
